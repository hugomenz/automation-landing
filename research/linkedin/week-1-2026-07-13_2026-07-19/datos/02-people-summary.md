## Missing evidence & limitations
- **Activity is `unknown` for most buyers** — role-search profiles were not opened to verify 30/60/90-day activity (conservative read-only budget). Verifying the top ~12 profiles' activity is the first Monday task.
- Company affiliations inferred from headlines need confirmation on the profile before any outreach.
- Post permalinks: only Prewa's is a verified canonical URL; others must be opened via the author's recent-activity during the week.
- No contact info was collected (by rule). No sensitive attributes inferred.
